Our Mission Statement

Cardinal Books is the independent, family-owned bookstore dedicated to serving the students, faculty, and Muncie community around the Ball State University campus since 1998. Our mission is threefold:

  1. To provide required textbooks and essential academic resources at the best possible value.
  2. To foster a love of reading and discovery by curating a diverse selection of new and used fiction and non-fiction titles.
  3. To create a welcoming, low-cost space where Cardinals can study, connect, and enjoy a great book, away from the stresses of campus life.
